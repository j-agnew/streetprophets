Street Prophets

Street Prophets is a new local act started by vocalist Jack Agnew and guitarists Jay Kim and Andy Patton. Drawing influence from Shoegaze, Punk, and Indie Rock, we aim to please the ears and put a new spin on the genres while we're at it. With a few small shows under our belts and a debut EP "Keeping Bad Company" on the way, we're preparing to take the next big step into the local Pittsburgh scene, and would love for you to join us.

Go ahead and listen to some of our music, look for upcoming shows, and access past show collections!
